Example

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) considers a flight to be delayed when it is 15 minutes later than its scheduled time.

flights <- mutate(
  flights, 
  delayed = case_when(
    dep_delay >= 15 ~ "Delayed", 
    dep_delay < 15 ~ "On time",
    .default = "other")
)
